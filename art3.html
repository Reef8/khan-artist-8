555: Eye on Ball

Inspired by Jahangir Khan (the G.O.A.T. of ssquash) and his remarkable 555 match win streak, that lasted nearly 5.5 years. This piece was created by hitting paint covered squash balls onto the canvas 555 times.A unique piece, that becomes truly mind blowing when you consider what each ball mark represents!

This piece displayed in its new home!

Displayed at the XBOX PSA World Tour Finals last year; one of the biggest squash tournaments in the world!!!
